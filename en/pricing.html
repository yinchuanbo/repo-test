    Vidqu Credit FAQs
    What can I do with the credits?
    You can use credits to translate videos in Vidqu AI Video Translator. If the translation doesn't take place successfully, the credits will be refunded automatically.
    Do my credits expire?
    While your subscription is active, your credits don't expire.
    If I cancel my plan, will the remaining credits be lost?
    If you cancel, the remaining credits disappear at the end of the current subscription period, not immediately upon cancellation. For example, you purchase a monthly plan on November 1, 2023. If you cancel your subscription on November 2, 2023, the remaining credits will disappear on December 1, 2023.
    Will unused credits be carried over to the next billing period?
    Yes, unused credits are accumulated and added to the new credits in the next billing cycle. For example, You purchase a monthly plan on November 1, 2023, and receive 80 credits. At the end of the current subscription period, there are 50 unused credits. For the next billing period, you will have a total of 130 credits (80 from the new subscription + 50 carried over from the previous period).
    What distinguishes a monthly, half-yearly, and yearly subscription?
    The monthly subscription charges every month, the half-yearly subscription charges every six months, and the annual subscription charges once a year.
